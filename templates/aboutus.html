About Us

What is Mentor Me?

Mentor me is an online platform for students of Joka to connect with their alumni across the globe.

Who uses our platform ?

This has exclusively been developed for IIM Calcutta community so that they can make informed career decisions

How does it work?

Two easy steps:-

1) Login using your updated LinkedIn profile

2) Connect with peers / seniors

Benefits for First Year students

• Understand different career options

• Know in-depth about internship experience

Benefits for Second year students

• Understand real job profiles / salary package

Benefits for Alumni

• Pay it forward by helping current IIM Calcutta students

• Know about referral openings in different firms

• Connect with peers / alumni to make career advancement / shifts
